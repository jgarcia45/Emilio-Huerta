How To Vote

Election Day is Tuesday, March 3, 2020

  • Check your registration status here.
  • Find your polling place here.
  • Vote at your polling place: Tuesday, March 3, between 7:00AM - 8:00PM

Vote Early in Person

  • Vote early at: The County Registrar's office located at 1115 Truxtun Ave. - 1st Floor, Bakersfield, CA 93301

Vote by Mail

  • Find vote-by-mail request form online here or by calling the County Registrar's office and return your mail ballot request form in by Feb. 25.
  • Fill out your ballot and return by mail or to the County Registrar's office *Sign, date, and fill out address.
  • Ballots must be postmarked by March 3rd.
